Terumo is Rising to the Challenges to Innovate in Seven Business Areas

Terumo contributes to society through healthcare by doing business through three companies with seven divisions. We provide optimal products and services to medical settings the world over. In pursuing higher quality in medicine, we introduce improvements for greater safety and ease of use, conduct training to promote proper use of devices, and propose systems for comprehensively meeting the needs of medical settings..

Cardiac and Vascular Company

Realizing minimally invasive treatments through endovascular interventions for various parts of the body and in cardiovascular surgery

General Hospital Company

Through unique technology and services, improving safety and efficiency in medical settings, while raising patient quality of life and innovating drug delivery

Blood and Cell Technologies Company

The Blood Management Company drives customer value and enhances patient outcomes through blood component, therapeutic apheresis, and cellular technologies
